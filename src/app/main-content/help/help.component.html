Manual

  • You can change diary entries and intervals while the timer is running.

Interval:

"Alert every":

  • You will hear a sound every x minutes and x seconds.

"Pre alert":

  • You will hear a reminder sound x minutes and x seconds before the main alert as a sign that it is almost time for you to get ready.

Note:

  • The "Pre Alert" must be shorter than the "Main Alert".

Diary sorting:

  • The bodyparts are sorted in this order: 'Chest', 'Back', 'Legs', 'Shoulders', 'Arms', 'Abdominal', 'Calves', 'Stamina', 'Other.

Diary Entry:

"Blue Button":

  • With the blue button you can insert the text from the "Info (Weights, ...)" field from the previous entry.
